About meal

This is a simple tasty meal. It is meant to serve 4.
Takes 10 minutes to prep and get everything ready.
As well as takes 1 hour 10 minutes to cook.



Nutrition per serving

Calories 407
Protein 30.9g
Carbohydrates 61.5g
Fat 4.4g
Cholesterol 68mg
Sodium 434.5mg

Ingredients needed

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 6 potatoes, quartered
  • 2 green bell peppers, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon-pepper seasoning


Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Place chicken breasts into a medium baking dish.
    Add potatoes, bell peppers, onion, water, and butter.
    Sprinkle lemon-pepper seasoning over the top.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken breasts are
    no longer pink in the centers and juices run clear, about
    1 hour and 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted
    into the center should read at least 165 degrees
    F (74 degrees C).

  4. Lasty just plate the meal however you like with
    any drink and sides you want and enjoy the meal.
